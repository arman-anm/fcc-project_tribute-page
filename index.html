Tribute to

Dr. Abdullah Jahangir

Dr. Khandakar Abdullah Jahangir (1958 - 2016) was a Bangladeshi islamic scholar and preacher. He was predominantly known for his work on the revival of the sunnah.

He taught his followers the importance of sticking to the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him) and not judging another muslim solely because of any fiqhi difference.
Many mainstream islamic scholars are now embracing this approach after realizing the importance it.

Dr. Abdullah Jahangir wrote many books on Islam. But he grew tremendous popularity specially anongst the youth from his YouTube channel.

On May 2016, this great soul died from a car accident. May Allah have mercy on him.
